Demon Slayer

Tanjiro Kamado

Tanjiro Kamado has a hard skull,
superhuman sense of smell,
the ability to enter the
Transparent World, repetitive
action, the ability to change
the colors of his Nichirin
Swords, and various breathing styles.

Zenitsu Agatsuma

Zenitsu possesses a heightened
sense of hearing, allowing
him to detect danger from
even the faintest of sounds.
With this, he has displayed
the ability to decipher layers
upon layers of sound in
one of the brothels and
even in the Infinity Castle.

Inosuke Hashibira

Inosuke is extremely flexible,
to the point where he
can bend backwards so
far that his head reaches
between his feet. He
later displays the ability
to dislocate his arm to
extend his reach and
dislocate any of the
joints in his body at will.
